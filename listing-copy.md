## Full description
Most inventory tools forecast a product. Apparel doesn't sell that way — you
run out of Medium Black while XXL White sits as dead stock. Restocked forecasts
demand for every variant (size and color), then tells you exactly how much of
each to reorder and when.

Built for apparel, footwear and accessory brands that reorder from suppliers.

What you get:
- Variant-level demand forecasting — seasonality, trend, and stockout-aware
  (sold-out weeks count as demand, not zero, so bestsellers aren't under-ordered).
- Reorder recommendations ranked by urgency, with safety stock and reorder points.
- Adjustable supplier lead time — retune the plan in one click.
- "Show your math" on every recommendation, so you can trust the number.
- One-click CSV export to send straight to your supplier.
- A weekly email digest of what needs reordering.

A clean replacement for spreadsheets — and for Shopify's retiring Stocky app.

## Key benefits (bullets for the listing)
- Reorder the right quantity of every size, not a flat split
- Catch sold-out bestsellers before you lose the sale
- Cut dead stock and end-of-season markdowns
- Trustworthy, explainable forecasts — no black box
